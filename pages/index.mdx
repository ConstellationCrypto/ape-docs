# What is ApeChain?
ApeChain is an advanced L3 blockchain tailored for gaming, designed to be the cornerstone of the Ape community. It offers high performance, ultra-low fees, and a specialized infrastructure for upcoming games within the ApeCoin ecosystem. By leveraging Caldera and Arbitrum's custom native token feature, ApeChain will utilize $APE as its native gas token. This integration significantly enhances $APE's utility, fostering a robust and dynamic economy driven by $APE, and positioning ApeChain as a premier platform.
